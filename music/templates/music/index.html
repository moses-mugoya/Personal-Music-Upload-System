About Us


MKD is an upcoming music group currently based in Kibra. Our main aim is to spread the word of God and inspire people through our music. MKD is composed of four people who are devoted to the group to ensure the main aim is achieved; Moses Mugoya aka Mugonchez is the vocalist, Derrick Kimaisi aka Trapper_ Kimdrick is a rapper, Daniel Wandera aka Danny B is a rapper too and finally George Juma aka Vajom who is also a rapper. MKD started in the year 2015 consisting of three members; Mugonchez, Kimdrick and Danny B. The group was named after the three members as initials of the first letter of their names,(Mugonchez Kimdrick Danny B). The group grew and now it has four members including Vajom.
